# Product

## Register

product

## Users

AJN ERP is used by Arabic-speaking operational staff, HR/payroll officers, finance teams, and managers, often from mobile devices while handling live business operations. Each screen should make the next valid action obvious and safe.

## Product Purpose

AJN centralizes the company’s operations, finance, HR, payroll, attendance, and executive reporting. Success means reliable financial and employee records, clear approvals, and fast access to the source of every reported figure.

## Brand Personality

Practical, dependable, and composed. The interface communicates confident operational control without visual clutter.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboards, dense unexplained controls, generic browser dialogs, and workflows that conceal consequential actions or financial state.

## Design Principles

- Make the current task and its status immediately clear.
- Preserve traceability from dashboards to their source records.
- Make financial and approval actions explicit, reversible where appropriate, and auditable.
- Prefer compact, familiar controls over decoration.
- Keep every critical workflow usable in Arabic RTL on a phone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessibility expectations. Support reduced motion, clear focus states, responsive RTL layouts, dark mode, touch-friendly controls, and no hidden or clipped critical actions.
