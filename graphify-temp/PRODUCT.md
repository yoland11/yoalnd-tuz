# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Iraqi consumers and event-planning clients browsing and booking luxury event services (photography, kosha setup, gift arrangements) and purchasing décor products. Primarily mobile users in Iraq, Arabic-first. Also business staff operating the admin panel to manage orders, inventory, and service bookings.

## Product Purpose

AJN (مجموعة علي جان / Ali Jan Group) is a luxury events and services business platform based in Tuz Khurmatu, Iraq. It serves as the full digital presence: customer-facing store and service booking site, plus an internal admin panel for order and content management. Success means customers can browse, book, and track orders confidently in Arabic, and staff can process and manage the business end-to-end without friction.

## Brand Personality

Opulent, trustworthy, warm — gold luxury with Iraqi cultural authenticity. Not cold or corporate. The brand conveys celebratory occasion, not everyday commerce.

## Anti-references

- Generic Shopify template aesthetics (flat white, gray-heavy, commodity feel)
- Western tech-startup minimal (cold grays, no warmth, no occasion feel)
- Cluttered bazaar-style Arabic e-commerce sites (overcrowded, low-contrast, no hierarchy)

## Design Principles

1. **Occasion-first**: Every screen should feel appropriate for someone planning the most important moments of their life — weddings, engagements, graduations.
2. **Gold is trust**: The primary accent (#D4AF37) is used exclusively for primary actions, active states, and brand moments. Never decorative filler.
3. **RTL is first-class**: Arabic typography, right-to-left layout, and Iraqi dialect copy are the default — not an afterthought.
4. **The UI disappears into the task**: For tracking, checkout, and admin, the product should be invisible. Users complete their job without thinking about the interface.
5. **Consistency over surprise**: Same component vocabulary across all screens. Delight in moments, not pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Focus indicators visible (gold ring). Arabic RTL, Kurdish and Turkish via i18n. Reduced motion supported globally. Mobile-first touch targets (44×44px minimum).
